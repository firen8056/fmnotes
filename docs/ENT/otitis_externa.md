# Otitis externa 外耳炎

### Classification

 - Acute：< 6 wks
 - Subacute：6-12 wk
 - Chronic：> 3 months

### Etiology and pathogenesis

- Pathogens ~(1)~
    - 最常見為細菌感染：PsA、S.A
    - fungus 也有可能 (otomycosis)：aspergillus、candia
    - virus：HSV、VZV

### Epidemiology and risk factors

- Epidemiology ~(1)~
    - incidence 10%，increased in summer，often after trauma or swimming
- Risk factors ~(1)~
    - Disease：eczema、poor removal of ear wax、narrow auditory canal
    - Others：swimmer、trauma、hearing aid
    - Complicated AOE risk factors
        - DM、immunocompromise
        - cranial radiotherapy or radiation

### Clinical presentation

- Symptoms ~(1)~
    - otalgia、itching、fullness (rapid onset < 48 hrs)
    - Other symptoms：hearing loss、jaw pain、vertigo
- Signs ~(1)~
    - pinna tenderness、ear canal edema or erythema、otorrhea、tympanic membrane erythema
    - regional LAD、cellulitis
- Complication ~(1)~
    - middle ear disease、osteitis、abscess
    - Red flag：headache or pain out of proportion、fever、granulation tissue at bony cartilage junction → 可能是 necrotizing infection → 可能有生命危險，要去急診

### Diagnosis and workup

- Diagnosis：藉由 ==臨床症狀== 診斷 ~(2)~
    - rapid onset (<48 hrs) + ear canal inflammation symptoms and signs
- Workup ~(2)~
    - otoscopy (+ pneumatic otoscopy)
    - ear canal culture
    - 若懷疑 malignant otitis externa，要考慮做 image 如 CT 等
    - 若有 granulation tissue 可考慮 biopsy

### Management, prognosis and follow up

- 生活型態調整：保持耳道清潔，移除異物和耳垢。keep ear dry for 7D ~(3)~
- 藥物
    - Tarivid 10gtt QD-BID for 7-14 day
    - Mycomb ear drop 2-3gtt TID-QID 7D
        - 耳滴劑使用方式：患耳朝上，點藥水，tragal massage + 朝上 5 mins ~(1)~
    - 其他可考慮口服止痛藥物 ~(3)~
- Prognosis
    - 症狀痊癒需2周，但48-72小時應好轉。~(3)~
- Follow up
    - if no improvement within 3 days → refer to ENT
