# Pompholyx (Dyshidrotic Eczema) 汗皰疹

### Etiology and pathogenesis

- 可能是由多種不同原因引起的非特異性免疫反應，但其確切機制尚不清楚

### Epidemiology and risk factors

- Epidemiology
    - 0.05 - 10.6%
- Risk factors
    - atopic dermatitis
    - contact dermatitis：反覆洗手、鎳、鉻、鈷、化妝品、洗髮精、香味、橡皮
    - smoking
    - stress
    - warm weathers
    - hyperhydrosis
    - fungal infection
    - drug reaction

### Clinical presentation

- Typical area：hands and feet，通常雙手對稱，有 vesicle，有些可能融合成 bullae，搔抓後可能會破裂

### Diagnosis and workup

- Diagnosis
    - clinical diagnosis
- Workup
    - 一般的情況下不需要

### Management、prognosis and follow up

- Management
    - general
        - 避免已知的過敏原或刺激物
        - emollient
    - topical agents
        - high potency steroid
        - pimecrolimus and tacrolimus if long-term treatment is needed
    - phototherapy
    - systemic
        - antihistamine
        - 嚴重可考慮 systemic steroid：如 prednisone 70mg，每天 taper 5-10mg，持續 1-2 week
        - 其他包括免疫調節劑、生物製劑、口服 A 酸等
- Prognosis
    - 可能會造成慢性持續復發
