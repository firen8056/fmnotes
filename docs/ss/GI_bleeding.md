# GI bleeding 腸胃道出血

### Differential diagnosis

| 區域分類 | 可能原因 |
|-----------|-----------|
| 上消化道 | esophagitis、GERD、GU、DU、angiodysplasia、celiac、gastric or esophageal cancer |
| 下消化道 | colon polyp、diverticular bleeding、IBD、hemorrhoid、anal fissure、mesenteric ischemia、colon cancer |

### Approach

- Hx
    - 出血：onset、duration
    - 其他部位：epistaxis、hemoptysis、menses、hematuria
    - 合併症狀：abd pain、bowel habit change、fever、weight loss、anemia or volume depletion symptoms
    - 過去病史：prior GIB、PUD、diverticulosis、IBD、hemorrhoid、celiac disease、GI or aortic OP、liver disease、coagulopathy、malignancy
    - 藥物：alcohol、aspirin、NSAIDs、anti-PLT、anticoagulant、herbal
- PE
    - vital signs、apperance、anemia and volume depletion signs
    - abd exam、liver disease signs
    - rectal：mass、hemorrhoid、fissure、stool appearance and color
- Exam
    - Lab：CBCDC、iron profile、BMP、coagulate、LFTs、guaiac
    - 胃鏡、大腸鏡
    - 其他包括CTA或angiography等
