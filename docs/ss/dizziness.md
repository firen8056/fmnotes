# Dizziness 暈眩

### Introduction

- Definition：Acute：<3個月；chronic：>3個月

### Differential diagnosis

| 分類 | 次分類 | 疾病 / 狀況 |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Acute vestibular syndrome (days to weeks without asymptomatic period) | Post-exposure | trauma、medication |
|                           | Spontaneous | vestibular neuritis、stroke、demyelinating lesion、tumor |
| Episodic vestibular syndrome (intermittent episodes, last seconds to hours or days) | Triggered | orthostatic hypotension、BPPV |
|                              | Spontaneous | vestibular migraine、Meniere disease、TIA、vertebral artery dissection |
| Chronic vestibular syndrome (months or longer) | — | PPPD |


### Approach

- Hx
    - Onset (acute、chronic、Episodic)、duration (mins、hrs、days)、Trigger (head motion、positional、orthostatic…)、aggravating factors (motion、change position…)
    - Associated symptoms (N/V、hearing、tinnitus、headache、photophobia、phonophobia、palpitation)
    - CNS symptoms (N/V、diplopia、ataxia、paresis、gait、other neuro signs)
    - Past history (trauma、stroke risk factors、medications)
    - TITRaTE：Classify vestibular symptoms
        - Timing：onset、duration、evolution
        - Trigger：action、movement、situation
            - 其實 BPPV、Meniere disease、vestibular neuritis、vestibular migraine、PPPD 姿勢改變都會惡化
        - Target examination
- PE
    - Vital signs (HR、BP、orthostasis)
    - Heart：murmur、arrhythmia
    - Vestibular system
        - Peripheral：Ear (otoscopic exam、Hearing exam such as Weber / Rinne)、Fukuda test
        - Central
            - Cerebellar exam (ataxia such as FNF、HST、wide-based gait、tandem gait)
            - Eye movement (pursuit、saccade、nystagmus)
            - Longtract cross sign
            - Bulbar function (Pa-Ta-Ka、uvula、tongue)
        - HiNTs exam：用來判斷 central or peripheral vertigo (head impulse – nystagmus – test of skew)
            - Head impulse
                - Normal：eyes move smoothly in opposite direction of head (suggestive of CENTRAL vertigo)
                - Abnormal：patient cannot maintain eye contact with their visual stimulus > saccades to refocus on target (suggestive of PERIPHERAL vertigo)
            - Nystagmus
                - Normal：unidirectional horizontal nystagmus (PERIPHERAL)
                - Abnormal：vertical nystagmus, eccentric nystagmus (when patient looks to the right, nystagmus is also to the right), or direction changing nystagmus (CENTRAL)
            - Test of skew
                - Normal：eyes remain motionless after being uncovered (PERIPHERAL)
                - Abnormal：after being uncovered, eyes will demonstrate saccade movements (CENTRAL)
            - Summary
                - Peripheral vertigo: ABNORMAL head impulse test, NORMAL nystagmus, NORMAL test of skew
                - Central vertigo: NORMAL head impulse test, ABNORMAL nystagmus, ABNORMAL test of skew
    - Dix-Hallpike test / Supine roll test
    - Proprioception：Romberg test
- Exam
    - Lab：CBCDC、肝腎功能、Glu、電解質、甲狀腺功能、CRP?
    - Heart：ECG、heart echo
    - Autonomic：Tilting table、SSRI / RRIV
    - Ear and vestibular
        - PTA
        - electronystagmography / videonystagmography
        - Caloric test：診斷單側前庭功能低下 gold standard：冷水同側慢對側快、熱水同側快對側慢。左右兩側慢期速度差異 >25% 表示單側異常。
        - Vestibular function test
            - Rotary chair test：診斷雙側前庭功能低下 gold standard，但只能測 lateral canal。
            - Dynamic visual acuity test
            - VEMP
            - 感覺整合測試 SOT、動態平衡測試
    - Vascular exam：Carotid doppler、TCD
    - Others：NCV/EMG、EEG、BAEP、MRI if suspected central lesions
        - indication of brain image
            - New onset headache
            - Any central symptoms or signs
            - Acute deafness
            - With vascular risk factors
            - Spontaneous vertigo ≥ 24 hrs
            - Intact head impulse test
